SUNBAKE BACK TO SCHOOL 2021

The promoter is RCL Foods Sugar and Milling (Pty) Ltd and Paton Tupper (Pty) Ltd, which is contracted to promote this Competition (collectively “the Promoter”).

Any persons entering or participating in this Competition (“Participant”) accept and agree that by entering this Competition they have had an opportunity to read and consider these Terms and Conditions and that they are bound by these Terms and Conditions.

What is the prize ?

Learners stand a chance of winning:

  1. one (1) of 10 000 (Ten Thousand) vouchers to the value of R130 (one hundred and thirty rand) each redeemable for one (1) pair of Student Prince Synthetic School Shoes at Pep Stores. Should the value of the school shoes chosen be less than R130 (due to size of shoe), the balance of the value can only be used towards the purchase of a second pair of Student Prince Synthetic School Shoes. The total prize of 10 000 vouchers is worth R1.3 million (One Million Two Hundred Thousand Rand) in total. School shoes must be redeemed from PEP stores before 31st May 2021.
  2. a share of R500 000 (five hundred thousand rand) in airtime awarded in denominations of R5 each.
  3. Schools nominated by Participants (“Nominated Schools”) stand a chance to win one (1) of three (3) prefabricated classrooms to the value of R250 000 each. (“Prizes”).
  4. The Prizes are not transferrable and may not be exchanged for cash or any other items. The Promoter reserves the right to substitute the prizes with any other prize of comparable commercial value.

Upon entering, participants will be asked to nominate their school of choice. The Nominated Schools will then go into the draw to win one (1) of three (3) classrooms. USSD calls are charged at 20c for every 20 seconds on the USSD line. Free minutes do not apply.
