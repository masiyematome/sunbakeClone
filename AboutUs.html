About Us

Since our launch in 1990, Sunbake’s delicious products have become a vital part of South Africa’s favourite meal moments.

Millions of people associate the Sunbake brand and “Happiness in Every Bite” slogan with our great tasting range of breads, buns and rolls, baked to suit various lifestyles and tastes.

Each made from the finest ingredients, we invite you to make Sunbake products a part of your healthy, balanced diet.
